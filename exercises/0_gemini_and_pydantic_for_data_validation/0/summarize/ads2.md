**Job Title:** Engineering Operations Technician, Data Center Engineering Operations
**Company:** Amazon Data Services Sweden AB (AWS)
**Location:** Västerås, Sweden (and other Swedish locations)

**Summary:**
AWS is seeking an Engineering Operations Technician to ensure the continuous availability and reliability (better than 99.999% uptime) of its mission-critical data centers in Sweden.

**Key Responsibilities:**
*   Operation, maintenance, monitoring, and troubleshooting of electrical, mechanical, HVAC, and fire/life safety equipment.
*   Assisting with new equipment deployment (racks, cabling) and facility upgrades.
*   Coordinating vendor activities and performing site walk-throughs.
*   Acting as a first responder to critical events.

**Qualifications:**
*   2+ years of relevant electrical, mechanical, and/or controls experience (degree/trade preferred).
*   Ability to prioritize in a fast-paced environment.
*   Valid EU driving license.
*   Availability for 24x7 shift rotation.
*   *Preferred:* Data center or heavy industry experience, professional certifications, and knowledge of systems like chillers, AHUs, and backup generators.

AWS emphasizes a diverse, inclusive culture with opportunities for mentorship and career growth.
