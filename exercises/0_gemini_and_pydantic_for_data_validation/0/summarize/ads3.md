## Engineering Manager - NOBA Bank Group (Stockholm)

NOBA Bank Group is seeking an **Engineering Manager** in Stockholm to lead a team of 5 software engineers. This role is crucial for their Nordax brand's customer onboarding, specializing in secure loans.

You will play a pivotal role in:
*   **Leading a team** of 5 engineers, providing guidance, mentorship, and support.
*   **Driving daily deliveries** across the full stack (C#/.NET/.NET Core, modern JavaScript/React, SQL).
*   **Engaging in strategic discussions** with Product and Technology leadership to shape the tech landscape and ensure business impact.
*   **Providing technical leadership**, supporting team decisions, and upholding development best practices.
*   **Contributing to recruitment** and strengthening the engineering organization.

**We're looking for someone with:**
*   7+ years in C#/.NET/.NET Core software development.
*   3+ years with modern JavaScript frameworks (React preferred).
*   2+ years of formal leadership experience.
*   Strong proficiency in Microsoft's development stack and SQL.
*   Fluency in English.

The ideal candidate enjoys both technical development and leadership, valuing collaboration and continuous improvement within a supportive, growth-oriented culture.
