**Job Title:** Engineering Operations Technician, Data Center Engineering Operations
**Company:** Amazon Data Services Sweden AB (AWS Infrastructure Services)
**Location:** Västerås, Sweden (and other Swedish locations)

**Summary:**
Amazon is seeking a Data Center Engineering Operations Technician to ensure the high availability and reliability (99.999% uptime) of mission-critical data center infrastructure. The role involves operating, maintaining, monitoring, and troubleshooting electrical, mechanical, HVAC, and fire/life safety systems. Candidates need 2+ years of relevant electrical, mechanical, or controls experience, an EU driving license, and must be able to work 24x7 shift rotations.
